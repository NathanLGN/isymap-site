Solutions

Home > Solutions

Isymap offers modular solutions thanks to the combination of autonomous, intelligent, miniaturized, connected, geolocated sensors and a carrier adapted to the specificities of use.

Isymap designs the functional, mechanical and electronic design of its measurement modules, develops its high-performance transmission systems as well as its real-time data collection and processing software.

Isymap offers products developed in-house from design to industrialization as well as tailor-made solutions.

Our process

Our innovations serve the needs of our customers. We guarantee a solution adapted to each use.

Measure what ?

Sensor

Depending on your needs, ISYmap designs tailor-made sensors :

  • Radiological sensor
  • Chemical sensor
  • Gaz sensor
  • Sound sensor
  • Capteur sonore
  • Etc.

Measure how ?

Additionnal functional technologies

Our developments adapt to your most restrictive objectives in order to integrate relevant transmission, geolocation and on-board calculation technologies.

Additional features such as odometry, telemetry or even iTV can also be offered.

Measure in what context ?

Integration of constraints

Our solutions are designed to meet the most complex environments with secure transmission technologies that are resilient to interference, jamming and extreme conditions.

We design low energy consumption solutions allowing operation over several years.

Finally, we ensure that the mechanical and electronic design simplifies operational deployment (ergonomics, interfacing, maintainability, wireless, etc.).

Measure for what data(s) ?

Information processing and visualization software

Our solutions are accompanied by data processing and visualization software and applications that adapt to the needs expressed.

We develop applications for smartphones, tablets, watches and PCs that can be equipped with different operating systems such as Android, iOS, Linux, Windows and MacOS.

Our applications are local or in the Cloud.
