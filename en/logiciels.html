ISYmap

MAPPING - MEASURES - INTERPOLATION - REAL TIME

ISYmap allows to manage real time data streams from ISYmap connected or compatible sensors. This data is presented in the form of a map whose refresh frequency is chosen by the user. Values interpolated by kriging as well as additional features are aggregated.

Simple and intuitive to use, ISYmap allows you to quickly understand a situation and follow its evolution.
Additional features allow operators’dosimetry to be optimized during interventions.

Functionalities
  • - Interactive mapping
  • - Base map satellite or plan
  • - Differential mapping allowing the evolution of measured values to be visualized
  • - Kriging interpolation of point data
  • - Visualization of gamma spectra
  • - History and archiving of measures
  • - Dosimetry optimization
  • - Visualization of areas of interest or exclusion
  • - Open data format with import/export in .csv format
Applications
  • Iterative mapping for investigation or surveillance.
  • Visualization of the evolution of contamination or pollution.
  • Search for sources or discharge outlet.
  • Decision support and reduction of risk exposure for operators.
  • Grouping and classification of measurement data, creation of histories.

ISYpop

MEASURES - INVESTIGATIONS - REAL TIME - CONFIGURATION

ISYpop is the application for acquiring and configuring ISYmap devices available on PC (Windows and Linux), smartphone and tablet (Android).

Capable of communicating via Bluetooth and LoRa, it covers a wide range of investigation needs (dose rate, gamma spectrometry, mapping, etc.).

A smart watch version, ISYdose, is also available.

Fonctionalities
  • - Interactive mapping
  • - Immediate monitoring of the radiological state of an area
  • - Visualization of the evolution of the dose rate
  • - History and archiving of measurements
  • - Open data format with import/export in .csv format
Applications
  • Immediate monitoring of the radiological state of an area.
  • Visualization of the evolution of the dose rate.
  • Search for sources or discharge outlet.

ISYgo

AERIAL MEASUREMENTS - REAL TIME - PILOTING

ISYgo is a unified interface for drone piloting and radiological measurement.

This Android application is compatible with DJI drones and Flighters sensors.

Fonctionalities
  • - Interactive mapping
  • - Similar functions to DJI GO for piloting the drone
  • - Display and recording of the dose rate located by the drone’s GNSS
  • - Centimeter localization option by RTK depending on the drone
  • - Data transmission to ISYmap software for real-time remote mapping and monitoring
Applications
  • Indoor and outdoor aerial radiological investigations.
  • Investigation of casemates or waste storage areas.
