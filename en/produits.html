GLOBall

DROPABLE - CONNECTED - GEOLOCALIZED - MAPPING - REAL-TIME

The GLOBall is a radiological sensor (dose rate or gamma spectrometry) that is shock-resistant, waterproof, and dropable. It keeps operators away from risk, thus protecting their health and safety.

In number, they constitute a network of sensors that can be quickly deployed to monitor the radiological state and evolution of an area in real time.

Fonctionalities
  • - Secure long range communication
  • - Autonomous and robust system
  • - Long battery life
  • - Easy and quick deployment
  • - Adjustable drop height (protective covers)
  • - Remote sensor deployment
  • - Remotely configurable
Applications
  • Emergency response.
  • Radiological source search.
  • Pipe investigation.
  • Investigation of restricted access areas.
  • Temporary beacon in harsh environment.

Guard

CONNECTED - GEOLOCALIZED - MONITORING - REAL TIME - PORTABLE

The Guard is a portable beacon monitors the radiological status of an area and providing alerts when needed.

Quick and easy to set up, its deployment requires no cables or construction.

The beacon provides real time feedback locally to operators (dose rate display, visual and audible alerts) but also remotely to a centralized control station thanks to its high-performance transmission system.

Alerted of danger, operators work in complete safety thanks to this beacon

Fonctionalities
  • - Visual and audible danger indications
  • - Real-time alerts
  • - Secure long-range communication
  • - Remotely controllable thresholds and parameters
  • - Automatic (GNSS) or manual positioning
  • - Wired or wireless connection
  • - Battery or mains powered
  • - Data history and traceability
Applications
  • Deployment of a radiological beacons on a temporary work site.
  • Automatic measurement of recurring control points.
  • Zoning monitoring for storage areas, zone boundaries, and site barriers.
  • Decision support to reduce the risk of operator exposure.

Flighter

CONNECTED - GEOLOCALIZED - AERIAL MAPPING - REAL TIME

The Flighter is a sensor that allows aerial radiological investigations to be carried out easily and quikly.

Very lightweight, it is adaptable to any type of drone, even the smallest.

Fonctionalities
  • - Real-time measurements
  • - Lightweight and miniature module
  • - Autonomous and independent of the drone
  • - GNSS position of the measurements
  • - Secure long range communication
  • - Quick and easy installation
  • - Measurement integrated into the drone control system
Applications
  • Periodic radiological mapping of storage areas for radiological waste drums.
  • Radiological mapping and monitoring in post-accident situations.
  • Investigation of inaccessible areas (decomissioning, etc.).

Safer

RISK MANAGEMENT - CRISIS MANAGEMENT - THREAT DETECTION - SITE SECURITY - REAL TIME - WIRELESS - LOCALIZATION - MAPPING

The Safer system enhances surveillance of an area to detect a potential radiological threat. Wireless, quick and easy to deploy, it has its own secure data transmission network.

It provides real-time remote feedback to the centralized control station thanks to its high-performance transmission system.

Deployed in large numbers or individually, it enables perimeter surveillance of sensitive facilities, monitoring of major events, recurring hot spot measurements, and more.

Fonctionalities
  • - Discreet, easily deployable sensor
  • - Real time measurements and alerts
  • - Secure long-range communication
  • - Remotely controllable thresholds and parameters
  • - Wireless
  • - Very long battery life
  • - Data history and traceability
Applications
  • Deployment of a sensor network for enhanced surveillance.
  • Automatic and recurring measurement of control points.
  • Zoning monitoring for storage areas, zone boundaries or even site barriers.
  • Early warning system.

ISYroll

MINIATURE - ROBUST - AGILE - WIRELESS - VISUAL INSPECTION - RADIOLOGICAL MAPPING

The ISYroll is a miniature, lightweight, wireless inspection robot equipped with an ultra-high-definition camera.

Optionnaly, it can be equipped with a radiological sensor (gamma spectrometry/dose rate).

Highly mobile and equipped with high-performance communication technologies, it is capable of navigating, via remote control, in areas with restricted access and in environments where radio transmissions are normally restricted (nuclear facilities, dam, etc.).

Fonctionalities
  • - Miniature
  • - Real time measurements
  • - Visual inspection with 4K (UHD) camera
  • - Radiological mapping
  • - Agile and robust
  • - Simple and quick deployment
  • - Wireless control
  • - IP67
Applications
  • Inspection of restricted access areas.
  • Inspection of industrial facilities/pipes.
  • Radiological source research.
  • Survey of dose rate measurement points.

Inspector

VISUAL INSPECTION - RADIOLOGICAL MEASURES - REAL TIME - ALL-TERRAIN

The Inspector is an all-terrain, wireless inspection robot equipped with an ultra-high-definition camera and a long range communication system (up to 10 km).

Optionnaly, it can be equipped with our lightweight radiological sensor, the Flighter (dose rate/ gamma spectrometry).

Fonctionalities

  • - Wireless
  • - Modularity of equipment (radiological sensor)

  • - Cost-effective, “consumable” material
Applications

  • Emergency response.
  • Optionnaly, radiological measures.
  • Investigation of casemates or waste storage areas.
