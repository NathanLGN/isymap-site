This document defines the cookie use policy of isymap.bcnco.fr.

A cookie is a file that is placed on your terminal (computer, mobile device, etc.). On the occasion of your visit to the website of isymap.bcnco.fr, it collects and stores information likely to improve the progress of your visits, to know your browsing habits or needs in order to be able to adapt to you. Finally, there are also cookies which allow us to carry out audience analyzes and detect navigation problems in order to monitor and improve the quality of your navigation on the site isymap.bcnco.fr.

This Cookie Use Policy applies to any user who visits the website of isymap.bcnco.fr, who fills out data collection forms. By continuing to browse the website of isymap.bcnco.fr, you accept without reservation this Cookie Use Policy.

The cookies used on this website are:

Cookies used by isymap.bcnco.fr pursue different goals; their validity period varies:
Classified according to their origin (1), their purpose (2) or their expiration time (3).

1. Origin of cookies

Proprietary cookies: deposited and managed directly by isymap.bcnco.fr.
Third-party cookies: deposited and managed by partners in order to carry out statistical analyzes of navigation on the site isymap.bcnco.fr. These analyzes are carried out completely anonymously.

2. Purpose of cookies

Technical and/or personalization cookies: by identifying the session, they facilitate navigation, thus allowing access to tools with restricted access. They make it possible to provide the service previously requested by the user.

Analysis and/or targeted advertising cookies: they allow audience statistics to be produced – number of visits received in the different sections of the site, frequency of visits, user behaviors and habits in order to improve navigation. (isymap.bcnco.fr uses Google Analytics, a web analysis service from Google Inc.). These cookies operate anonymously.

3. Cookie expiration time

Session cookies: collect and store data when the user browses the website isymap.bcnco.fr.
Persistent cookies: collect and store data on the user’s terminal for a variable duration, depending on their purpose.

Even if you configure your browser so that all or some cookies are refused or blocked, you will continue to access information from the website isymap.bcnco.fr. However, deactivating one or more cookies could prevent or make navigation on the site difficult or limit the use of certain services offered by isymap.bcnco.fr. Once you have given your consent to place one or more cookies, you can withdraw it at any time and the cookie(s) will be deleted.

isymap.bcnco.fr  reserves the right to modify this Cookie Use Policy when changes to the configuration and/or use of cookies take place and will publish the updated version on its website.

Attention, taking your different wishes into account is based on a Cookie or several specific Cookies. If you delete all the Cookies stored on your terminal concerning our site, we will no longer know what consent or refusal you have given. This will therefore amount to resetting your consent and you will therefore have to once again refuse the Cookie(s) that you do not wish to keep. Likewise, if you use another Internet browser, you will have to refuse these Cookies again because your choices, like the cookies to which they relate, depend on the browser and the terminal (computer, tablet, smartphone, etc.) that you use to consult our site.
