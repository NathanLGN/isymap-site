About us

Home > About us

ISYmap’s ambition is to become the reference in innovative solutions for measuring and processing information.

Thanks to its expertise in developing miniaturized wireless sensors and embedded system software, ISYmap’s objective is to provide its customers with solutions to measure better, be better informed and therefore make better decisions.

Since its creation, ISYmap has maintained a constant dynamic of innovation, developed its patented technologies and signed technical and commercial collaborations and partnerships.

ISYmap is also concerned about the quality of life at work for all members of its team and is committed to a human resources policy promoting inclusion and diversity within the company. In this sense, a 2024-2026 Gender Equality Plan has been produced.
