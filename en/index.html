Discover

Since 2016, ISYmap (Instrumentation SYstems for MAPping) has been developing and marketing connected measuring instruments as well as intelligent robots for traditional to severe environments, presenting a risk for humans and/or access constraints.

All of the products offered can be or are equipped with miniature radiological or chemical sensors allowing a wide range of investigations.

The objective of these solutions is in particular to distance operators from risk and to secure their intervention.
ISYmap therefore works on the industrial markets, particularly nuclear, and security-defense.

The technologies developed by ISYmap are patented in France and internationally.
